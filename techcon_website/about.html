History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation, bringing together the brightest minds in the tech industry. From groundbreaking product launches to life-changing keynote speeches, TechCon has become synonymous with technological progress.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire and empower individuals and organizations to innovate, collaborate, and lead in the ever-evolving tech landscape. Our commitment to diversity, sustainability, and creativity drives every aspect of the conference.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed some of the most influential figures in technology:

  • Ada Lovelace - Known as the first programmer, Ada's visionary contributions laid the foundation for modern computing.
  • Alan Turing - A pioneer of artificial intelligence, Alan's work continues to influence the tech industry today.
  • Grace Hopper - A programming legend, Grace developed the first compiler and made coding accessible to millions.
